Budgeting Tools

We provide two tools, monthly and yearly tools. Pick the one you like.
Monthly Budgeting Tool

This Budgeting tools allows you to create a spending plan for your money, it ensures that you will have enough money for your top priorities which you can assign in the app. Budget plan will also keep you out of debt or help you work your way out of debt if you currently in debt.

Key terms for the tool:

  • Priority expenses: Expenses that are mandatory for every month.

  • Level of necessary: Each expense can be divided in terms of the level of necessary.

  • Monthly available capital: Amount available every month.

  • ­Excepted savings every month: Total money goes into savings from every month.
