Budgeting Tools

We provide two tools, monthly and yearly tools. Pick the one you like.
Monthly Budgeting Tool

This Budgeting tools allows you to create a spending plan for your money after visualizing your monthly spending. User can prioritize their spending by getting an instant feedback on how a particular expense affects their budget! This tool is helpful for people hoping to keep you out of debt

Key terms in the tool:

  • Budget: Your budget or spending money for the month.

  • Expense: Amount of money you spent on an activity in a particular month.

  • Balance: Amount of money left after subtracting your total expenses from your monthly budget.

Yearly Budgeting Tool

This Budgeting tools allows you to create a spending plan for your money, it ensures that you will have enough money for your top priorities which you can assign in the app. Budget plan will also keep you out of debt or help you work your way out of debt if you currently in debt.
