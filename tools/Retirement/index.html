General Introduction

Welcome to the Retirement Calculation Tool.

Enter the amount of money you want when you retire, as well as your monthly contribution and expected growth of investment (APY). We will then provide you with the expected timeframe of your retirement.

Usage

This calculator provides users with insights into their trajectory for retirement.

Method

We begin by depositing the initial balance into the account. At this point, we simulate waiting a month. Next, we calculate the interest gained during the period, which is represented by: Interest = Balance * (1+ (APY/12))

Finally, we add the interest earned during the period and check to see if we have reached our retirement goal. If not, we wait another month and check again.
